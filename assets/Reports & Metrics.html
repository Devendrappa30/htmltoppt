Detailed Reports & Metrics

Here you'll find comprehensive reports and key metrics that I utilize to track project progress, team performance, and overall Agile health.

  • **Burn-down Charts:** Visualizing work remaining in a sprint or release.
  • **Velocity Charts:** Tracking the amount of work a team can complete in a sprint.
  • **Cumulative Flow Diagrams (CFD):** Showing work in progress, completed work, and incoming work.
  • **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) Scores:** Measuring customer happiness with delivered features.
  • **Lead Time & Cycle Time:** Understanding the efficiency of the development process.

These metrics enable data-driven decision-making and foster continuous improvement.
